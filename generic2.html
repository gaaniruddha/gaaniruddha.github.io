Humans of PES

I was the admin of our university's unofficial Facebook page called "Humans of PES" inspired by Humans of New York. .

  • This page was started by my senior at PES Uni sometime in 2015. He was kind enough to pass it on to me at the beginning of January 2017, before he graduated in May 2017.
  • It was a really fun job! The task was to go around the campus, talk to students, learn about their interesting/fun stories and write them for everyone to read. My identity as the admin had to be confidential.
  • In my brief tenure as the admin from January 2017 to May 2018, I managed to publish around 11 articles. I started by interviewing my then-best friend and then went on to interview newer students.
  • Shortly before entering my final year in August 2018, I passed on the baton to my juniors, and the page still continues.
