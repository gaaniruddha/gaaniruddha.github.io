Humans of PES

I was the admin of our university's unofficial Facebook page called "Humans of PES" inspired by Humans of New York.

  • The page was initially created by a senior at PES University in 2015. He generously passed it on to me at the start of January 2017, just before his graduation in May 2017.
  • It was an incredibly enjoyable role! My task was to roam around campus, engage with students, hear their interesting and fun stories, and write them up for everyone to read. Throughout this time, I had to keep my identity as the admin confidential.
  • During my brief tenure as admin, from January 2017 to May 2018, I had the privilege of publishing around 11 articles. I began by interviewing my then-best friend and later expanded to newer students.
  • Just before entering my final year in August 2018, I passed the baton to my juniors, and the page continues to thrive today.
